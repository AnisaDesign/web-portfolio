Linhan Interiors & Co.

Rebranding & Thumbnail designs

As part of the rebranding for LINHAN Interior Design, I refreshed the company’s visual identity to feel more modern, minimal, and elegant reflecting the high-end nature of their services. The updated look was tailored for their target audience of clients aged 25–50 seeking luxury residential renovations and custom-designed commercial spaces. Additionally, I created a set of custom YouTube thumbnails to support LINHAN’s video content. Each graphic was thoughtfully designed to catch the viewer’s eye while maintaining brand consistency. Clear layouts, engaging imagery, and concise titles helped make the videos more approachable and professional.

BUSiNESS CARDS & BROCHURE

Using a palette of pale pink, purple, and blue colors requested by the client, I designed clean, modern business cards that gave each contact a distinct and elevated feel.

YOUTUBE THUMBNAIL DESiGN

I created these thumbnail graphics for my client’s Youtube channel. The goal for each thumbnail was to grab people’ attention and draw them into our channel. The right size, engaging and high-quality photo, graphics and title text were used to give our viewers some context.

WALL DECAL

I was requested to create a Wall Decal design using the brand name for the new office my client moved in with her team. My client was inspired by the works of American artist and designer, called "KAWS" and the works of American conceptual artist and collagist "Barbara Kruger". My goal was to reflect and combine both styles into one modern and bold typography for Linhan Design brand name wall decal.
