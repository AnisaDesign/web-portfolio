Linhan Interiors & Co.

Rebranding & Thumbnail designs

As part of the rebranding for LINHAN Interior Design, I refreshed the company’s visual identity to feel more modern, minimal, and elegant reflecting the high-end nature of their services. The updated look was tailored for their target audience of clients aged 25–50 seeking luxury residential renovations and custom-designed commercial spaces. Additionally, I created a set of custom YouTube thumbnails to support LINHAN’s video content. Each graphic was thoughtfully designed to catch the viewer’s eye while maintaining brand consistency. Clear layouts, engaging imagery, and concise titles helped make the videos more approachable and professional.
