HELLO THERE!

I’m Anisa, a curious, creative UX/UI and Product designer based in Vancouver, with a calm presence and a love for clean, thoughtful design. My journey began in graphic design, and recently I completed my diploma in Interaction Design at Capilano University, where I dove deeper into creating meaningful, user-centered digital experiences. I’m drawn to clarity, simplicity, and purpose whether I’m refining a user flow or shaping the look and feel of an interface. My work blends visual design with UX research to build intuitive products that feel both efficient and aligned with brand values. This website? I built it myself from scratch using HTML and CSS. A small example of how I enjoy merging design thinking with front-end development to bring ideas to life.
