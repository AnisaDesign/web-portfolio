Yannie Ma

Client Logo Design (Freelance)

This logo and business card design were created for photographer Yannie Ma, whose style is modern, classic, youthful, and minimalistic. Her target audience primarily includes women aged 20–50. Yannie requested a logo that was simple and minimal while also reflecting an Asian aesthetic to honor her Chinese heritage. Red was chosen as the primary color, symbolizing luck and energy in Chinese culture, and giving the logo a bold, memorable look.

MOCKUPS

The logo balances a serious, reliable feel with an inviting touch, using bright red for strong visibility and rounded corners to create a friendlier appearance. This adaptable design works well across various platforms, including her photography, personal website, social media, layouts, and publishing materials, ensuring a consistent and professional brand presence.
