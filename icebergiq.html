THE CHALLENGE

I conducted a UX audit of key pages to identify friction points related to navigation, hierarchy, and content clarity, then translated these findings into clear recommendations that guided the redesign.

THE APPROACH

The redesign focused on simplifying navigation, strengthening visual hierarchy, and reducing cognitive load across the home and about pages to clearly communicate IcebergIQ’s value and guide users toward key actions. I also refreshed the visual system by introducing two additional brand colors and designing industry-specific icons to improve clarity and consistency.
