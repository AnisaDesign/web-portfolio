LOGiiX
(Atlantia Co.)

Packaging | Campaign Design | Mailer | Digital Ads

LOGiiX is a tech accessories brand under Atlantia, focused on creating colourful, functional products that make everyday technology more enjoyable and accessible. As part of the marketing team, I contributed to a wide range of creative initiatives, including packaging design, seasonal campaigns, product photography, and digital marketing assets.

PACKAGiNG

Reporting to the Director of Marketing, I designed packaging for new product launches and reorders, supporting the process from concept to production. My responsibilities included product photography and retouching, dieline creation and updates, icon and illustration design, varnish detailing, product copywriting, and preparing production-ready files.

PRODUCT PHOTOGRAPHY

As part of my work at LOGiiX, I created product photography to support both packaging and marketing. I was involved in styling, lighting, and capturing images that clearly showed product features while staying consistent with the brand’s colourful and modern look. I also edited and retouched the photos so they could be used across digital campaigns, packaging, and promotional materials.

CAMPAIGN DESiGNS

I worked on monthly marketing campaigns that supported product launches, promotions, and seasonal initiatives. I created visual assets across digital and print, including web banners, social media, email graphics, and in-store materials. My focus was on maintaining brand consistency while adapting the visuals to different channels and audiences.
