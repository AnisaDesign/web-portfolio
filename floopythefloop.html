Floopy

Web and Motion Designs

Floopy the Floopy began as a character created by my daughter in 2022, and I later turned it into a series of stickers. Inspired to take this project further, I developed Floopy into a character with multiple themes—original, Christmas, and Easter—each with its own story, unfolding as part of a children’s e-book series. Floopy is a curious, lively, and playful character, bringing joy and excitement to every adventure.
