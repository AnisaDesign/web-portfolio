Spring Sale

Product Photography

This campaign highlights my skills in product photography. I was asked to photograph both new and existing products that were on sale at the time. Spring is often associated with cherry blossoms and blooming flowers, and after getting approval from the marketing team, I decided to incorporate floral elements into the shoot. Some of the flowers were purchased from a store, while the leaves and branches were gathered from a nearby local park. My main goal was to showcase the key features of each product, leave enough negative space for text placement, and capture the viewer’s attention through the use of bright, spring-inspired colors.
