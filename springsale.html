Spring Sale

Product Photography

This campaign showcases my skills in photoshooting. I was requested to photoshoot the new and old products that were on sale that time. The spring is usually associates with cherry blossom and flowers. After getting approval from the marketing team, I decided to go with flowers and photoshoot the items with the flowers on the background. Some of the flowers were purchased from the store, while leaves with branches were picked from the local park nearby. My main goal was to reflect the main features of the product, leave some space for the text and attract viewersâ€™ attention by the bright spring colours of plants and flowers.
